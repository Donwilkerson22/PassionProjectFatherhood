Don's World: Fatherhood Edition

Donovan Wilkerson April 11, 2023, Web droid Binary Coding Class Spring 2023

I was introduced to this passion officially on May 14, 2022 when my daughter Imarah was born. Since early childhood, I was fascinated and motivated by my strong and tight knit family. Even as a youngster I knew that I wanted to continue my family legacy by being a father and have children of my own. Today I am inspired by my own parents. They have always done all they can do to pour greatness into me. My Mom: a strong minded and stern woman that sets the bar for the standard of conduct and behavior. My Dad: A hard working and integral man who is a constant reminder to do things right the first time and to reach down and lift others up. I am also inspired by the other fathers I meet who share valuable experiences and virtues.
